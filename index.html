Infix To Postfix/Prefix Converter

The way to write arithmetic expression is known as a notation. An arithmetic expression
can be written in three different but equivalent notations, i.e., without changing the essence
or output of an expression.
These notations are :

  • Infix Notation
  • Prefix (Polish) Notation
  • Postfix (Reverse-Polish) Notation
    •

Infix expression: The expression of the form a operator b. When an operator is in-between
every pair of operands.

Postfix expression: The expression of the form a b operator. When an operator is followed
for every pair of operands.

Prefix expression: The expression of the form operator a b. When an operator is preceded
for every pair of operands.

For using the below converter just follow these simple steps:
-Firstly, select the button indicating the conversion i.e InfixToPrefix (or) InfixToPostfix
-Secondly, enter the infix expression in respective text box
-Finally, by clicking the Get postfix/prefix expression button you will get the desired result.
